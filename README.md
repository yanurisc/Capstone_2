# Capstone 2: Business Analysis with Python


# Project Overview
EmporiUm Sales Territory Analysis

This project analyzes in store sales data for EmporiUm, a growing student bookstore chain that also sells tech gear, art supplies, and other products. I will be analyzing sales data for your two territories, looking at store performance, monthly revenue trends, top customers, and product category breakdowns. The goal is to end with a marketing recommendation for where EmporiUm should focus attention next quarter. 

I will be comparing sales performance across two assigned cross-region territories:

Territory 1 - Massachusetts | Territory Manager: Bo Heap | Region: Northeast

Territory 2 - New York | Territory Manager: See Ellefson | Region: East

# Database Tables Used
The analysis includes monthly revenue, store rankings, top customers, and product category trends, with a final marketing recommendation.

# Sales Analysis Questions
1. Who are the territory managers and what stores are in each territory?
2. What is the monthly total revenue for each territory over the full data period?
3. How do stores rank by sales performance within each territory?
4. Who are the top rewards customers in each territory?
5. What are the transaction counts and revenue by product category?
6. What is the recommended marketing focus for next quarter?
